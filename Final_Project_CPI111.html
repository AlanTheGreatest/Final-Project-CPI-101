The pandemic that we are currently living through has shown us that using face and mouth coverings is a good way to stop the spread and the liklihood of contacting COVID-19.
That is why I have created this website with a simple tutorial to show you how to make a mask with only a t-shirt and a few easy steps.
This comes in handy when you run out of disposible masks or all your cool masks are dirty and in need of a wash.
Get a stylish shirt
Flip the shirt inside out
Put your head through shirt but not all the way with tag facing down
Tie sleeves behind your head
Make sure the sleeves are tight
It should look like this from the back
Just a simple knot will do
Pull top down as prefered
Cover mouth with the bottom, and tuck tag away
